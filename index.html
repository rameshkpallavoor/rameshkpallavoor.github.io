Short Bio

I graduated with a PhD from the Department of Computer Science at Boston University where I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Sofya Raskhodnikova. From 2014 to 2017, I was at Pennsylvania State University, where I was a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering before transferring to BU with my advisor. My primary areas of interest include Sublinear Algorithms and Differential Privacy.

I graduated from IIITD&M Kancheepuram in May 2014 with a Bachelor of Technology (B. Tech) degree in Computer Engineering.
